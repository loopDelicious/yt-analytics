Caveats & methodology
  • Selection effect: if the team reserves stronger content for certain days, the day's average reflects content quality, not day-of-week.
  • Small samples: days with <3 videos are faded and should be treated as directional only.
  • Channel growth: daily views have grown substantially over the export window, so "8-day post-publish" totals skew toward recent publishes. Without a pre-publish baseline this measures correlation, not causation.
  • Channel-wide, not per-video: the launch curve uses total channel views, because per-video daily views aren't available from the API. Catalog views dominate the 8-day totals.
  • Lifetime views confound age: older videos have had more time to accumulate views. Day-of-week rankings don't adjust for video age.
